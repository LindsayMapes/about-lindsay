From the Pages of "Lindsay and Brizo"

This is a story about the WARRIOR Princess Lindsay and her two closest friends; CETO and BRIZO.

Once upon a time, the ancient Princess Lindsay was riding her beloved Shark, CETO (Keto). CETO was born with a rare gift of lasers. These laser helped Lindsay protect her land and people. One day on patrol, Lindsay noticed a strange and dark black smoke in the distance. CETO and her swam up to investigate.

As they approached the smoke they realized it was coming from a strange yet beautiful creature. A creature never seen before with sweet eyes, powerful wings and breath of fire. Lindsay approached this strange creature, slow and careful. The creature spoke and called herself Brizo. She was an ancient beast that had lived many millennia; alone, hunted, and sad. Lindsay told her that she was no threat and that she would help protect her with the aid of her trusted companion, CETO.

Brizo, CETO and Lindsay were an unforgettable site. The Warrior Princess and her friends spent all day and all night together, protecting the villages, and living life. They would roam the mountains, seas and air and have the greatest adventures anyone could ever ask for.
